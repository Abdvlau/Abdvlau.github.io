How It Works

Step 1

Register your organization and employees.

Step 2

Employees check in and out using their devices.

Step 3

Monitor attendance and generate reports.

Student Step

Register and then log in as a student to sign attendance for a particular course with date and time.

Staff Step

Register and log in as staff to see the number of students that signed attendance for a particular course. Also, retrieve attendance using the filter function for filtering by date and time.

About Us

Inspiration Story

The idea for Scanly came from our collective frustration with the inefficiencies and inaccuracies of existing attendance system solutions. We wanted to create a user-friendly, accurate, and reliable attendance app. We noticed that many attendance systems were difficult to use and often made mistakes in tracking who was present. We wanted to create a user-friendly, accurate, and reliable attendance app that anyone could use easily Our inspiration for Scanly came from a common frustration we all experienced with current attendance systems. These systems were often hard to use and made mistakes in tracking who was present. We knew there had to be a better way, so we set out to create an app that would be easy for anyone to use, while also being accurate and reliable. Our goal was to eliminate the headaches and errors associated with traditional attendance tracking, making the process smooth and efficient for everyone involved

Our Mission

We are a dedicated team passionate about improving attendance management processes. Our mission is to provide an efficient and reliable solution for organizations to track and manage attendance seamlessly.
